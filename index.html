You Are Not Alone.
Your pain is real. This moment will pass.
Step 1 — Stop & Breathe
Breathe in for 4 seconds. Hold for 4. Out for 6. Repeat 4 times. This activates your parasympathetic nervous system and reduces pain perception.
Step 2 — First Line Medication
Check your medication log. Take your prescribed rescue medication.
Step 3 — Environment
Reduce stimulation: dim light, quiet, comfortable position. Apply heat or cold to painful area. Use headphones with calming audio if helpful.
